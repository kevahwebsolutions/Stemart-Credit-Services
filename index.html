Stemar Credit Services is a microcredit company based in Kenya with an aim of empowering micro and small businesses.

Every Kenyan deserves access to transparent and accessible credit services. Stemar Credit is committed to providing excellent and essential financial solutions to everyone, especially employees and employers.

SME loans

Stemar Credit Services supports SMEs with loans for working capital, expansion, and inventory. Enjoy flexible terms and competitive rates to empower your business growth.

Check off loans

We offers Check Off Loans, which are deducted directly from your salary. These loans provide convenient and timely financial assistance with flexible repayment terms, ensuring you have the funds when you need them most.

School fees loans

Stemar also offers School Fees Loans to help cover educational expenses. Our loans provide timely financial support with flexible repayment terms, ensuring that your child's education is never interrupted.

Logbook Loans

Stemar Credit Services offers Logbook Loans, using your vehicle as collateral. Get quick access to funds with flexible repayment options, while still retaining the use of your car.
